LIFE IS HARD

Life is beautiful but not always easy: it has problems,too,and the challenge lies in facing them with courage,
letting the beauty of life act like a balm, which makes the pain bearable,
during trying times,providing hope
Happiness,sorrow,victory,defeat,day-night,are the two sides of me coin.
Similarly life is full of moments of joy,pleasure,success and comfort punctuated by misery,defeat,failures and problems
There is no human being on earth ,strong,powerful,wise or rich,who has not experienced,struggle,suffering or failure.
